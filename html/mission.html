WHY BLACKPEN

Hello my name is Phillip Watson ive been a designer for 3 years. BlackPen is aproject started with the intention developing a design philosophy through specifying in UX/UI, Web and Editorial Design. The core of this philosophy being the idea of essentialism; the right content, in the right place, at the right time.


Effective design shapes the aesthetics, interactions and experiences of our world. I will use design to explore philosophical questions of how and why we live the way we do and convince people to reflect on an individual level.




I WILL PROVIDE DESIGN DIRECTION BASED ON THE CAREFUL
ANALYSIS OF USER DATA AND PRODUCT GOALS.

WHY BLACKPEN

Hello my name is Phillip Watson, a designer. BlackPen is a project started with the intention developing a design philosophy through practicing a cobination of UX/UI, Web, Editorial, Film & Fashon Design. While these projects have been developing for some time the project began in earnist in March of 2020, when I lost my job due to the COVID-19 pandemic better known as the Carona Virus.




Effective design shapes the aesthetics, interactions and experiences of our world. I will use design to explore philosophical questions of how and why we live the way we do. With this I will convince people to reflect on an individual level. My experiences have trained me to deliver content a skill that lies at the heart of design.

The idea of essentialism; the right content, in the right place, at the right time.

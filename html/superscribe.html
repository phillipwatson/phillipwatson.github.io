Superscribe

Superscribe guides writers as they finishing their first, second and final draft. Superscribe allows a writer to develop and organize their writing using prewriting tools. Once a story starts to takes shape it begins to write itself!

UI

From brainstorming, outlining, graphing, pre-writing and writing the information is contained all in one app.Writer can reference pre written information without leaving the writing page!

The story circle is a simple diagram of the hero's journey, the path that a character takes through their story. This is a guide so think of every story as a combination of these elements and not necessarily all of them.

Writing a story is a journey and story beats are the must see stops along the way. They can be as brief or detailed as you need. Press the create button below to add a beat and it will be added to the timeline. You can edit and reorder the beats as you go.
